I am a PhD candidate (dissertator) in the Department of Risk Management and Insurance at Peking University's School of Economics, with an expected graduation in 2026.

Prior to joining Peking University, I completed my undergraduate work in Actuarial Science at the University of International Business and Economics in June 2021.

I was a visiting scholar in the Department of Risk and Insurance at the University of Wisconsin-Madison's Wisconsin School of Business, from September 2024 to March 2025.

Research Interests: Insurance, Public Economics, Behavioral Economics
